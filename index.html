The Problem

Millions of African students complete secondary school without a clear career direction — unaware of the academic pathways, scholarships, or global opportunities available to them.

That gap costs them years of potential. NextGen Access embeds structured career guidance directly into the school experience, so no student steps into the world without a plan.

What We Offer

Programs Built Around Every Student

From SAT prep to psychological support, every service is designed to give students the tools and confidence they need to thrive.

🎓

Scholarship Coaching

Identifying regional and global scholarship opportunities and coaching students on essays, documentation, and matching them to the right funding.

🌍

University Application Support

Step-by-step guidance through personal statements, document preparation, timelines, and requirements for local and international universities.

🧭

Career Guidance & Counseling

One-on-one sessions, psychometric assessments, subject selection guidance, and career awareness workshops tailored to each student's strengths.

📐

SAT & College Readiness

Structured SAT instruction covering Reading, Writing, and Math — with regular tests, practice sessions, and full SAT Centre management.

💡

Entrepreneurship & Skills

Hands-on entrepreneurship training, student innovation projects, and workshops in public speaking, teamwork, and problem-solving.

🧠

Psychological Support

Individual counseling sessions addressing personal, emotional, and academic challenges — with early identification of students who need extra care.

🏆

Leadership & Community Service

Designing service projects, tracking service hours for university applications, and leadership development workshops for future changemakers.

🎪

Career Fairs & Events

Hosting school and regional career fairs with universities, industry experts, panel discussions, and seminars that open real-world doors.

How It Works

Our structured model integrates seamlessly into the school environment, delivering measurable outcomes at every stage.

01

Psychometric assessments and one-on-one counseling sessions uncover each student's strengths, interests, and goals.

02
